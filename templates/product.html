CARE TIPS

SHINE PRESERVATION: We recommend that the jewelry be removed before going to bed or participating in extreme physical activities. Exposure to certain harsh chemicals or environments will result in compromising the radiance of your jewelry. This includes, but is not limited to the following: perspiration, perfumes, cleaning agents, chlorine, salt water, and silver polish.

CLEANING: Using a small, slightly damp, soft cloth lightly polish your jewelry until the shine has visibly improved. If the dirt is hard to remove, soak the jewelry in clean water. Gold plated jewelry requires more care, using cloth roughly may cause the gold plating to fade away, therefore it is very important that this process is done gently to avoid the gold plating from wearing away.

STORAGE: Every order from us comes with our very own signature Viea pouch that is designed with a special microfiber fabric especially made to preserve the jewelry quality as well as for safe and secure storage of your jewelry. Store your jewelry in our signature Viea pouch, away from humid places, direct sunlight and heat.

NOTE: Gold plated and Gold Vermeil pieces are, by default, more fragile and liable to lose their colour over time. The speed at which our jewelry fades will depend on multiple variables such as skincare products, level of perspiration or even your natural skin. However, Viea pieces can last for a long time, years at that, once specially cared for. For this reason, it is particularly important to take special care of your jewelry to ensure a quality that lasts.
